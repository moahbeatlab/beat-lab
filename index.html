Beat Lab — Help

Transport

▶ PLAY / ■ STOPStart or stop the sequencer. Also: Space
BPMTempo slider (90–160 BPM)
SWINGGlobal swing amount. Delays odd-numbered 16th notes. Activate per track with the ↪ button.
VOLUMEMaster output volume
STEPSPattern length: 16 or 32 steps
CLEARClear all active steps
TAP TEMPOTap repeatedly to set BPM
SP-404 SHEETOpens a pattern reference sheet with velocity and micro-timing values for programming the Roland SP-404 MK2
SHARECopies a shareable link to your clipboard. Anyone opening the link gets your exact pattern loaded.

Step Grid

ClickActivate step, cycling through velocities: 90 → 100 → 110 → 127 → off
Drag left/rightPaint mode — activate multiple steps at the same velocity across a track
Alt + drag left/rightErase mode — remove steps as you drag across them
Drag up/downFine velocity control on a single step (drag up = louder, down = quieter)
Right-clickRemove a single step immediately

Track Controls

Track nameClick to mute/unmute. Double-click to rename.
VOL sliderPer-track volume
↪ (SW)Toggle swing on this track. Click the SW header to toggle all tracks at once.
ATK/DEC ▼Expand the envelope & EQ panel for this track

Envelope & EQ Panel

SoundSynthesizer type: kick, snare, hat (closed/open), shaker
TunePitch / base frequency of the sound
Attack / DecayEnvelope shape. Short decay = punchy, long = roomy.
HP FreqHigh-pass filter — cuts low end. Useful for thinning out hats or snares.
Low Shelf / High ShelfBoost or cut low and high frequencies (±12 dB)

Presets & Library

PresetsLoad a built-in drum pattern inspired by different house music styles
+ My LibrarySave the current pattern to your local library (stored in browser)
LOADRestore a saved pattern from your library
SAVEOverwrite a library slot with the current pattern
DELDelete a library slot
